Whenever Writing a blog, Name the .html file as blogddmmyyb.html

Where:

blog	: Write as it is
dd	: Date of writng of Blog
mm	: Month of writng of Blog
yy	: Year of writng of Blog % 100
b	: Blog number for the day - Refer to the repository at https://github.com/CodeBlazers17/CodeBlazers17.github.io for the list of blogs posted on that day and number your blog accordingly

 The Title of the blogpost must be clear and concise
